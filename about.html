About

We are a family owned business and have been since 1993! It is our goal to fulfill your Lawn and Landscaping needs, and to provide a professional job which you are bound to enjoy. Decker Lawn & Landscaping is a Family owned business that has been servicing the Northland for over 20 years! Other places include: Kearney,Gladstone, Pleasant Valley, Claycomo, Kansas City North, but are not limited to these locations. Just give us a Call. We are experienced professionals that get the job done right the first time. We have the ability to work with the homeowner and get the work done in a manner that fits their needs.

Bob and Christina Decker have been happily married for more than 22 years. Taking trips with their children to many different places in the United States and Europe are the things they enjoy in life. Bob and Christina are both graduates of Liberty High School. Being able to support their children during school has been one of the biggest bonuses of owning their own business. They have been able to attend all events in which the kids participated in. Now that both kids are college bound they are now able to accommodate more landscaping needs. They couldn’t have asked for more flexibility. Bob and Christina take pride in the fact if you call and need assistance from them you will speak with them, the work is complete by the family and they all have the same pride in knowing that we give 100% to make the customer happy.
